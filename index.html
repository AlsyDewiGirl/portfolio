About Me

Hello, saya Alfi Syah Dewi Risgi

I’m a Software Engineering student at SMKN 2 Bekasi with a strong passion for technology and problem-solving. I have a great interest in web development and IT systems, and I always strive to expand my knowledge by applying it to real-world projects.

During my 2.5-month internship at PT. Elsewedy Electric Indonesia, I gained valuable experience in both the Warehouse and IT divisions. My responsibilities included document filing, renaming and scanning documents, as well as contributing to web-based projects such as improving the asset borrowing system and developing a visitor management website for the security team. This experience enhanced my technical expertise while strengthening my adaptability, teamwork, and attention to detail.

I am committed to continuous professional growth, focusing on building efficient and scalable solutions while sharpening my skills in both software development and IT support.
